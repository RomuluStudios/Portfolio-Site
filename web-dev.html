GradePilot

Link To Github Repo


GradePilot is more than just a student planner. The core idea behind this project is to remove the stress of due dates, assist in planning what to work on and make students lives easier through a smart, adaptive Priority Engine.

Feature Walkthrough

Adding Schedule

Users are able to add their work schedule, choose its frequency (weekly, bi-weekly or monthly) and choose whether that is a permanent or changing schedule.


In addition to the users work schedule, they can also add their class schedule as well as any one-off appointments they have.


Importing Courses

Users are able to upload course CSI documents, and autofill course information such as grading categories (assignments, labs, etc.) as well as the specific items within those categories. This uses Paddle OCR through a cloud based server, and uploaded images are deleted after parsing and user confirmation.
This allows users to ensure OCR accuracy before importing information, in addition to the ability to edit courses themselves from the course screen.
